Where to go from here

I hope you have enjoyed this tour through the Strongtalk system. Please feel free to browse through the system and borrow anything interesting you see. We built this system because we wanted to take Smalltalk to the next level, and we would love to see people take advantage of what we accomplished.

Other things that you might want to read:

  • Here is a little more history on the Strongtalk system and the technologies and team that contributed to it.
  • You might want to read the Release Notes, which show how the releases differ, and also point out bugs and shortcomings.
  • If you have any problems running the system, try reading the Troubleshooting page.

Thanks for your interest in the Strongtalk system!
